# Dark Classy

## Visual Direction
Dark Classy establishes premium dark mode as a presentation idiom — not gamer-dark or developer-terminal dark, but the dark of a premium product keynote or a luxury brand presentation. Near-black surfaces are layered in two distinct values (#0A0A0B for the base, #141417 for elevated cards and panels) to create depth without any shadow effects. The warm vibrant orange accent (#FF5C00) operates with gradient variation — from a pure orange to a deeper amber — creating energy and warmth against the cool dark foundation. Instrument Serif headlines bring editorial prestige to slide titles, a deliberate counterpoint to the dark-tech context. DM Mono handles metrics with clinical precision. Luminous green status dots signal positive data points. This style excels in product launches, startup investor decks, premium B2B platforms, and any presentation designed to communicate innovation and ambition.

## Color Palette

| Role | Hex | Description |
|------|-----|-------------|
| Background | #0A0A0B | Deep near-black — the foundation layer |
| Surface | #141417 | Elevated surface — cards, panels, containers |
| Text Primary | #FFFFFF | Pure white for maximum contrast on dark |
| Text Secondary | #ADADB0 | Cool mid-gray for supporting copy |
| Accent | #FF5C00 | Warm vibrant orange — CTAs, key metrics, highlights |
| Success | #22C55E | Luminous green — positive status, growth indicators |

## Typography

### Display Font
- **Family:** Instrument Serif
- **Weights:** 400 (regular) — Instrument Serif's elegance lives in its regular weight
- **Size Range:** 56–72px for title slides, 36–48px for section headers
- **Letter Spacing:** -1px at display sizes; 0px at section header sizes
- **Style Notes:** Italic variant for pull quotes and single key phrases. Instrument Serif should appear exclusively on title slides and section openers — not on body content slides. The contrast between serif titles and sans-serif body is the editorial signature.

### Body Font
- **Family:** Inter
- **Weights:** 400 (regular), 500 (medium), 600 (semibold)
- **Size Range:** 15–18px body, 13px supporting, 11px footnotes
- **Line Height:** 1.5 body, 1.3 lists and tight blocks

### Data/Mono Font (if applicable)
- **Family:** DM Mono
- **Weights:** 500 (medium)
- **Usage:** All metrics, percentage callouts, KPI values, timestamps, and technical data in charts and tables. The slightly heavier 500 weight ensures legibility at small sizes against dark backgrounds.

## Design Principles
1. Layer depth through surface values, not shadows. The two-level surface system (#0A0A0B base, #141417 elevated) provides all the depth needed. Box shadows are prohibited — they break the premium dark aesthetic.
2. Orange is a warm signal in a cool field. The #FF5C00 accent should appear on the single most important element of each slide. For gradient variation, a linear transition from #FF5C00 to #CC4400 is permitted on large decorative elements only.
3. Instrument Serif titles establish editorial authority. Their presence on title and section slides elevates the presentation above generic dark-mode decks. Never use Instrument Serif for body copy or data labels.
4. Soft corner radius (6–12px) on all card and container elements. This is the only style in this collection that uses rounded corners — they are part of the premium product aesthetic and must be applied consistently.
5. Luminous status indicators carry meaning. Green (#22C55E) signals positive performance; use it only for genuinely positive metrics and growth arrows. Do not use it decoratively.

## Slide Application Notes
- **Title Slides:** #0A0A0B background. Title in Instrument Serif 400 at 64–72px, white, flush-left. Optional: one word in the title set in the orange accent. A short subtitle in Inter 400, 18px, #ADADB0. Presenter/date in DM Mono 500, 12px, #ADADB0, bottom-left.
- **Content Slides:** #141417 surface cards on #0A0A0B background. Cards with 8–12px corner radius. Section label in Inter 600, 11px, #ADADB0, uppercase, +0.5px tracking. Body in Inter 400, 16px, #FFFFFF. Key callout numbers in DM Mono 500, 32–48px, #FF5C00.
- **Data/Chart Slides:** Dark surface (#141417) for the chart container, 8px radius. Axis labels in DM Mono 500, 11px, #ADADB0. Primary data series in #FF5C00. Positive trend indicators in #22C55E. Gridlines in a 15% white opacity rule — never a solid line. Values in DM Mono 500, 13px.
- **Closing Slides:** Full #0A0A0B background. Instrument Serif 400 closing statement at 56px, white. Orange accent on one phrase. DM Mono contact details, 12px, #ADADB0, bottom-left.
- **Do:** Apply 6–12px radius consistently to all cards. Use orange for single high-priority elements. Layer #0A0A0B and #141417 to create depth.
- **Don't:** Add drop shadows. Use rounded corners inconsistently. Apply the orange accent to more than two elements per slide. Use white backgrounds or light surfaces.
