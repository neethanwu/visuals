# Elegant Luxury

## Visual Direction
Elegant Luxury is a dark opulence system — the visual language of premium fashion houses, private equity firms, and luxury brand presentations. The near-black backgrounds (#0F0F0F for the main canvas, #0A0A0A for sidebar and accent panels) create a depth through subtle tone variation rather than shadows. Champagne gold (#C9A962) is the singular accent — used with extreme restraint, it carries aspirational weight precisely because it appears so infrequently. Warm cream text (#FAF8F5) reads softer than pure white against the dark backgrounds, giving the palette a tactile quality. Playfair Display carries the aspirational emotional register at headline level while Manrope handles all functional content with quiet authority. Zero decoration, zero corner radius, hairline borders — the restraint is the luxury.

## Color Palette

| Role | Hex | Description |
|------|-----|-------------|
| Background | #0F0F0F | Near-black main canvas — slightly warm undertone |
| Sidebar / Panel | #0A0A0A | Deeper near-black for secondary surfaces |
| Text Primary | #FAF8F5 | Warm cream — softer and more luxurious than pure white |
| Text Secondary | #888888 | Medium gray for supporting content |
| Accent | #C9A962 | Champagne gold — the singular accent, used sparingly |

## Typography

### Display Font
- **Family:** Playfair Display
- **Weights:** 400 (regular) — never bold; luxury communicates through refinement, not force
- **Size Range:** 56–72px for title slides, 36–48px for section headers, 24–32px for slide subtitles
- **Letter Spacing:** 0px default; +1px on premium labels and logo lockups only
- **Style Notes:** Italic is the prestige variant — use Playfair Display italic for pull quotes, campaign phrases, and single key words within a title. The roman regular carries authority; italic carries aspiration.

### Body Font
- **Family:** Manrope
- **Weights:** 400 (regular), 500 (medium), 600 (semibold)
- **Size Range:** 15–18px body copy, 12–13px supporting, 11px footnotes and metadata
- **Line Height:** 1.6 for paragraphs, 1.4 for lists, 1.2 for tight label rows

### Data/Mono Font (if applicable)
- **Family:** Manrope (tabular numerals)
- **Weights:** 500 for standard metrics, 600 for hero KPIs
- **Usage:** Financial figures, percentage metrics, and data callouts all use Manrope with tabular numeral settings. The gold accent (#C9A962) is permitted on hero KPI numbers that represent a positive or premium business result.

## Design Principles
1. The champagne gold is the rarest element in the system. It should appear on at most one element per slide — a key number, a single word in a headline, a hairline rule, or a category marker. Never use it as a background fill or for body text.
2. Two surface levels create all the depth required. #0F0F0F and #0A0A0A are used to separate foreground content from background panels. No gradients, no shadows — the tonal difference does the structural work.
3. Zero corner radius on all elements. Sharp edges communicate precision and authority. Any rounding introduces a softness that conflicts with the luxury register.
4. Hairline borders in gold or at 20% cream opacity are the only permissible structural lines. A 1px #C9A962 rule beneath a title is a sophisticated accent; a 2px border around a card is not appropriate in this system.
5. Playfair Display is the emotion; Manrope is the function. They should never compete on the same hierarchical level. Playfair owns titles and pull quotes; Manrope owns everything else.

## Slide Application Notes
- **Title Slides:** #0F0F0F full-bleed background. Title in Playfair Display 400 at 64–72px, cream (#FAF8F5), flush-left, centered vertically in the upper half. A 1px gold hairline rule beneath the title, spanning 30–40% of slide width. Subtitle in Manrope 400, 18px, #888888. Presenter/date in Manrope 400, 11px, #888888, bottom-left.
- **Content Slides:** #0F0F0F background with #0A0A0A sidebar panel (20–25% slide width) for section navigation or supporting content. Section label in Manrope 600 uppercase, 10px, +1px tracking, #C9A962. Body in Manrope 400, 16px, #FAF8F5. Hairline gold rule separating sidebar from main content.
- **Data/Chart Slides:** Chart on #0A0A0A panel, flush to one side. Chart title in Manrope 600, 18px, cream. Primary data series in #C9A962. Secondary series in #888888. Axis labels in Manrope 400, 11px, #888888. Hero KPI in Manrope 600, 48–64px, #C9A962.
- **Closing Slides:** Return to full #0F0F0F. Playfair Display 400 italic closing phrase at 56–64px, cream. One key word or phrase in gold. A short Manrope 400 contact line, 13px, #888888, bottom-center or bottom-left.
- **Do:** Use gold with extreme restraint — one element per slide. Embrace the two-level dark surface system. Let Playfair italic carry the emotional moment.
- **Don't:** Round any corners. Use gradients or shadows. Apply gold to backgrounds or large fills. Mix the two typefaces on the same hierarchy level.
