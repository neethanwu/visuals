# Brutalist Luxury Dark

## Visual Direction
A deliberate contradiction: the raw, blunt force of Bebas Neue condensed ALL-CAPS at monumental scale, set against the refined italic warmth of Cormorant Garamond. The canvas is near-black (#0D0D0D) — not charcoal, not dark gray, but a deep, absorptive almost-black that swallows light and makes the matte gold (#C9A962) glow with restrained opulence. This is not a luxury system that polishes its surfaces; it leaves the concrete raw and places a single gold object at the center. Bebas Neue operates at display sizes (48–80px) for titles and section labels, always uppercase, often with extreme letter-spacing (+6px on labels). Cormorant Garamond italic brings editorial softness to body passages. Corner radius is zero across the entire system — no rounding, no softening, no concession to approachability.

## Color Palette

| Role | Hex | Description |
|------|-----|-------------|
| Background | #0D0D0D | Near-black — deep, absorptive primary canvas |
| Surface | #1A1A1A | Elevated card background, slightly lifted from the base |
| Elevated | #2A2A2A | Second-level elevation for nested cards or hover states |
| Text Primary | #F5F4F2 | Warm off-white — not pure white, which would be too harsh |
| Text Secondary | #8A8A8A | Mid-gray for supporting text and metadata |
| Accent | #C9A962 | Matte gold — the singular luxury signal in the system |

## Typography

### Display Font
- **Family:** Bebas Neue
- **Weights:** 400 (the only weight; Bebas is a single-weight condensed display face)
- **Size Range:** 64–80px for slide titles, 48–60px for section headings, 24–32px for card titles
- **Letter Spacing:** +6px on section labels (ALL-CAPS, wide-spaced for maximum air); -1px on large titles (80px+) to counteract optical spread; +1px on card titles
- **Style Notes:** Always uppercase — Bebas Neue has no true lowercase. At large sizes, allow the condensed letterforms to stack tall and dominate the slide. Use sparingly for body text; this is strictly a display face.

### Body Font
- **Family:** Cormorant Garamond
- **Weights:** 400 italic (body copy, editorial passages), 600 semibold (callouts, featured quotes, key statements)
- **Size Range:** 14–18px for body (italic 400), 20–28px for featured statements (semibold 600)
- **Line Height:** 1.7 for italic body (Garamond italic needs room to breathe), 1.4 for semibold callouts

### Data/Mono Font (if applicable)
- **Family:** Bebas Neue
- **Weights:** 400
- **Usage:** Large metric figures and KPIs. Set at 64–80px with -1px tracking. Gold color (#C9A962) for the single most critical metric per slide.

## Design Principles
1. The tension is the design — Bebas Neue's raw bluntness and Cormorant's refined italic exist in deliberate opposition; never let one dominate so completely that the other disappears.
2. Gold is singular — #C9A962 appears once per slide, maximum. It marks the single most important element: the key metric, the primary CTA, the featured quote.
3. Darkness is depth — the near-black canvas (#0D0D0D) is not a background but a material. Surface (#1A1A1A) and Elevated (#2A2A2A) layers create a physical depth without any shadow effects.
4. Letter-spacing is dramatic — +6px on ALL-CAPS labels creates monumental air; -1px on large titles keeps them dense and heavy. Both extremes are intentional.
5. Zero radius, always — any corner rounding would introduce a softness that directly contradicts the brutalist-luxury positioning.

## Slide Application Notes
- **Title Slides:** #0D0D0D canvas, Bebas Neue 72–80px title in #F5F4F2, gold (#C9A962) used for a single horizontal rule or the subtitle accent. Cormorant Garamond italic for subtitle or tagline at 18–22px.
- **Content Slides:** Surface cards (#1A1A1A) on base canvas. Bebas Neue ALL-CAPS section label (+6px spacing) in warm off-white. Body in Cormorant italic 14–16px. Gold reserved for the card's primary data point or featured figure.
- **Data/Chart Slides:** Bebas Neue 64–80px for key metrics in gold. Secondary figures in warm off-white. Cormorant semibold 600 for chart labels and data callouts. Use #2A2A2A (Elevated) for chart backgrounds to lift them from the slide surface.
- **Closing Slides:** Return to pure #0D0D0D canvas. Single Bebas Neue statement at maximum scale. Gold accent on the CTA or closing line. Cormorant italic tagline beneath.
- **Do:** Use the full 64–80px size range for Bebas Neue on title and data slides. Let the near-black absorb — don't fill the canvas with cards.
- **Don't:** Use gold on more than one element per slide. Apply any corner radius. Set body copy in Bebas Neue — it cannot function at reading sizes.
