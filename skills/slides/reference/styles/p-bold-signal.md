# Bold Signal

## Visual Direction
Bold Signal is the loudest style in the collection — not through decoration, but through sheer typographic scale and neon voltage. A near-black canvas (#0B0B0F) serves as negative space for condensed display type set at maximum impact: tall, tight, and demanding attention. The accent system uses a single neon tone — electric lime (#BFFF00) by default or hot orange (#FF6B00) as an alternate — never both at equal weight. The neon accent is the signal: it marks exactly one thing per slide as the most important element. Everything else is white or gray, receding to let the signal land. This style rejects subtlety and editorial restraint in favor of urgency, energy, and forward momentum. It belongs on stage at product launches, demo days, tech conferences, and any moment designed to make people lean forward.

## Color Palette

| Role | Hex | Description |
|------|-----|-------------|
| Background | #0B0B0F | Deep blue-black canvas — darker and cooler than pure black |
| Surface | #151519 | Elevated cards, containers, chart backgrounds |
| Elevated | #1F1F25 | Second-level surface for nested elements |
| Text Primary | #FFFFFF | Pure white — headlines and key content |
| Text Secondary | #8A8A96 | Cool gray — metadata, supporting copy, captions |
| Accent | #BFFF00 | Electric neon lime — the signal. CTAs, key metrics, emphasis |
| Accent Alt | #FF6B00 | Hot orange — secondary accent or alternate lead color |
| Border | #2A2A32 | Subtle cool borders for structural separation |

**Accent usage rule:** Choose either lime or orange as the lead accent for the entire deck. The other may appear sparingly (e.g., a secondary data series) but never at equal visual weight. One signal, not two.

## Typography

### Display Font
- **Family:** Bebas Neue (or Barlow Condensed 700 as alternative)
- **Weights:** 400 (Bebas Neue is single-weight; Barlow Condensed uses 700)
- **Size Range:** 80–120px for title slides, 56–72px for section headings, 36–48px for slide titles
- **Letter Spacing:** -1px at 80px+; 0px at 56px; condensed faces already read tight
- **Style Notes:** ALL-CAPS always. Bebas Neue is inherently uppercase. The extreme vertical proportion of condensed type creates drama at large sizes — lean into this. Titles should feel like they're shouting from a billboard, not whispering from a book.

### Body Font
- **Family:** Manrope (or Inter as alternative)
- **Weights:** 400 (body), 500 (medium labels), 700 (bold callouts)
- **Size Range:** 16–20px body, 13–14px supporting, 11–12px captions
- **Line Height:** 1.5 for body, 1.3 for tight label stacks

### Data/Mono Font
- **Family:** Space Mono (or JetBrains Mono)
- **Weights:** 400 (data labels), 700 (hero metrics)
- **Usage:** All metrics, KPIs, percentages, timestamps, and technical data. Hero metrics in accent color at 56–80px. Data labels in uppercase 11–12px with +1px tracking.

## Design Principles
1. Typography IS the design. No decorative elements, illustrations, or ornamental borders. The condensed display type at extreme scale is the visual event. If you need decoration, your type isn't big enough.
2. One neon signal per slide. The accent color marks the single most important element — a metric, a headline word, a CTA. If everything glows, nothing signals. White and gray carry the rest.
3. Dark canvas is negative space, not a surface. The near-black background should feel vast and empty around the type. Generous padding (80px+) lets the content breathe and the neon accent pop.
4. Zero radius, zero shadows, zero gradients on content. Hard edges and flat surfaces keep the energy raw. The only permitted gradient is a subtle glow behind the neon accent text (text-shadow or radial gradient at very low opacity, max 10%).
5. Speed over elegance. Transitions should be fast (200-300ms). Animations should snap, not ease. This style moves like a countdown, not a waltz.

## Slide Application Notes
- **Title Slides:** Full #0B0B0F canvas. Bebas Neue at 96–120px, white, ALL-CAPS, flush-left or centered. One word or phrase in the accent color. Subtitle in Manrope 400 at 18–20px, #8A8A96. No decorative elements — just type on dark.
- **Content Slides:** #0B0B0F canvas. Section label in Space Mono 400, 12px, ALL-CAPS, accent color, +1.5px tracking, top-left. Heading in Bebas Neue 56–72px, white. Body in Manrope 400, 18px, white. Key number or stat in accent at 48–64px. Surface cards (#151519) with #2A2A32 borders for grouped content.
- **Data/Chart Slides:** Surface container (#151519) for charts with #2A2A32 border. Primary data series in accent color. Secondary in #8A8A96. Gridlines at 8% white opacity. Axis labels in Space Mono 400, 11px, #8A8A96. Hero KPI above the chart in Space Mono 700, accent color, 56–72px.
- **Closing Slides:** Full #0B0B0F canvas. Bebas Neue at 80–96px, white, centered. CTA or key phrase in accent color. Contact in Space Mono 400, 14px, #8A8A96, centered below with generous spacing.
- **Do:** Push display type to extreme sizes (96px+). Use the accent color with surgical precision — one element per slide. Leave generous negative space (80px+ padding). Keep transitions fast and sharp.
- **Don't:** Use both lime and orange at equal weight. Add decorative elements, icons, or illustrations (let type carry everything). Use rounded corners or soft shadows. Slow down the animations — this style is fast.
