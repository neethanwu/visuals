05 — Philosophy

Restraint is
a feature

The skill knows when not to add things. Most decks need clean typography and clear structure — not particle fields and shader backgrounds.

Minimalist deck
Subtle fades. No effects. Typography carries it.
Corporate quarterly
Clean charts, styled tables. Zero visual noise.
Tech product demo
WebGL particle field, neon glow, shader background. Full effects.
